About

SUYOP is a free website that showcases impressive extracurriculars for high school students in Toronto. The selection is handpicked to provide quality experience and to add to your resume and applications.

Whether you're a student looking for a volunteering opportunity overseas, an engineering program over the summer, or a sports league weekly, SUYOP is the perfect place to find all your needs.
